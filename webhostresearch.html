Web Host Research

By: Robert Hereth

Below are three different web hosting services and a brief overview of the three services. Following the three services, there is a short summary of the service I chose and the reasons why I chose it. Enjoy!

Note: The prices shown are for a '.com' domain with the name jeffspartyworld.com.

GoDaddy

Cost: $11.99 /yr

Features:

  • "Award-Winning" Customer Service
  • Over 62 Million Domains Hosted
  • Only offers shared hosting with "99.9% uptime" guarantee
  • 1-Click install of applications for website

BlueHost

Cost: $11.99 /yr

Features:

  • Offers dedicated or shared hosting on monthly price
  • Around 2 million domains hosted
  • Large push on utilization of WordPress
  • 24/7 support

Weebly

Cost: $19.95 /yr

Features:

  • Free, integrated webiste builder
  • Focus on E-Commerce sites due to Square integration
  • Integrated annalytics
  • Mobile app for checking statistics

My Choice: GoDaddy

Though GoDaddy doesn't have the best record when it comes to commericals, they seem to be the best choice for hosting a website due to their low cost and lesser push on what services you can add. BlueHost, and especially Weebly, push their other extentions, tools, and services more than their web hosting services. In addition, Jeff's Party World is a site that can only be hosted by a ludicrously named service like GoDaddy.
