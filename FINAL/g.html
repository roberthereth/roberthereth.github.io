G Rated Films

Below are my seven favorite G rated films. Any and all of these films would be a great watch for a family movie night, especially during this quarantine!


Ratatouille

Pixar's Ratatouille is, in my opinion, one of their best films. Throughout the film, the mainstaple Pixar ideas are explored: family, self discovery, and the human experiance. Ratatouille offers one of the best Pixar experiances out there. Many film reviewers revere Ratatouille as the top Pixar film due to its humor, visuals, and touching story. I can not suggest this film enough, especially with a young audience. Parents, be aware that knives and winer are shown throughout the film, but not in any visceral or inherently inappropriate ways.


Toy Story (All Four)

Ever since I was young, the Toy Story films captivated me. The premice was playful (pun intended) and wonderfully executed by the voices of Buzz and Woody, Tim Allen and Tom Hanks. Combined with the cast of relatable and creative characters, all films in the Toy Story franchise are great for kids. Adults, know that Toy Story 3 and Toy Story 4 will make you cry. :)


Yellow Submarine

This film is an unusual inclusion. Though this film doesn't feel aimed towards younger audiances, the psychadelic visuals and wonderfuly used music make this Beatles film a unique experiance from start to end. I first watched Yellow Submarine on VHS at the age of three. My parent's love for The Beatles pushed me to see this film. This film isn't the best for those under the age of 8, but parents and kids alike will fall in love with the music and odly charming storyline.


Overall Favorite: The Emperor's New Groove

The video above is one of my favorite reviews of The Emperor's New Groove. This film, much like the others above, was introduced to me at a young age. I always loved the characters, the comedy, the pacing, and the visuals of this film. It is, above all else, my favorite Disney movie. The Emperor's New Groove masterfully pairs meta humor, life lessons, and some of Disney's best voice acting to create my favorite G rated film. If you haven't seen this one, I cannot reccomend it enough.
