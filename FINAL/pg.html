PG Rated Films

This page contains four of my top PG movies. Most of these movies would be great for anyone over the age of 8. This list was my most difficult to compile, mostly due to the variety of styles and tones. These would also be a great viewing for a family movie night, but parents should watch them ahead of time and see if they are appropriate for their kids.


Spiderman: Into the Spiderverse

Spiderman: Into the Spiderverse is one of the best animated movies I have seen. They sheer style and look of theis movie is enough to make anyone go and see it. The art makes the movie feel like a 3D comic book, which is one of the coolest styles ever. Combined with the score, storyline, and countless references, Spiderman: Into the Spiderverse is one of the best "kids" films I have seen. This movie is a must see for any fan of comics, science, or movies, and it has something for everyone to enjoy.


Elf

Elf, starring Will Ferrel, is one of the cornerstones of the Christmas movie genre. This comedic take on the traditional Christmas scenario is one of the most quotable, funny, and feel good movies out there. This film has become a yearly watch at my house, and it will always hold a special place in my heart.


Monty Python and the Holy Grail

Though some scenes in Monty Python and the Holy Grail are more suited for a PG-13 rating, viewers of all ages will find something to enjoy in this comedic take on the medieval genre. Made by the British comedy troop Monty Python, Monty Python and the Holy Grail will go down in history as one of the innovaters in the comedy genre. The countless quotable scenes, hilarious writing, and slapstick humor make this film one of my all time favorites.


Overall Favorite: The Nightmare Before Christmas

Tim Burton's unforgettable The Nightmare Before Christmas combines two unlikely holidays into one genre-bending stopmotion masterpiece. The video above is the opening song, "This is Halloween", and it perfectly captures the movie in three and a half minutes. The beautiful visuals, haunting yet catchy soundtrack, and heartwarming story create an experiance like no other. The chilling imagery contrasts beautifully with the over the top characters and colorful world. This movie always gets two anual viewings at my household: once on Halloween and once on Christmas Eve. The morals of the story and the signature Tim Burton flair make this my favorte PG film of all time.
